About me, Robert Bernard Howell

Short Biography

I was born on November 29 th 1997 in Camden, New Jersey. I grew up in a small town named Woodstown, in Salem county. My high school was small, my graduating class was around 250 students.
At an early age I was always fascinated by machinery and technology, namely how they functioned. I would spend afternoons taking apart old clocks and radios and examining the circuit boards and resistors that were housed inside.
I have created this website to practice my HTML coding skills and learn how to implement CSS into webpages.
